About Me

Personal Trainer

I started personal training about 15 years ago. I never imagined the ways that it would eventually change my life. After years of schooling and working in various settings, I ventured out and opened up my own physical business. I was very fortunate to be successful with wonderful clients. I learned a lot and continue to learn more and more through multiple outlets. Perhaps the best source of knowledge, however, comes from working with clients. Their journey is my journey and this individual connection is vital. I also mentor and provide consultation services to clients across the U.S. for business purposes. Feel free to reach out to me if you'd like to know more.
